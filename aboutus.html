About Nieuw Carry Crafts

Company Info

Incepted in the year 2019 at Nellore (Andhra Pradesh, India), we “Nieuw Carry Crafts” are engaged in Manufacturing of non-woven, poly pack bags.

We have developed a huge infrastructural facility that is well-equipped with modern machinery, required for carrying large scale production. Furthermore, we had appointed a team of highly skilled and talented professionals, who are experienced in their domain. We design the offered range of bags in compliance with the latest market trends.

Nieuw Carry Crafts, established in 2019, is an Andhra Pradesh-based company.
